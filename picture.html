Health

1.Pathologists: Pathologists are medical doctors who specialize in diagnosing diseases by examining tissues, cells, and body fluids in a laboratory setting. They play a crucial role in laboratory health by analyzing samples and providing diagnostic interpretations to guide patient care. Pathologists often work closely with laboratory technologists and technicians to ensure accurate and timely results.

2.Test Ordering and Interpretation: Doctors in laboratory health may order specific laboratory tests based on a patient's symptoms, medical history, and clinical presentation. They interpret the results of these tests to aid in diagnosing diseases, monitoring treatment effectiveness, and assessing overall patient health. Doctors collaborate with laboratory professionals to understand the nuances of different tests and their implications.
